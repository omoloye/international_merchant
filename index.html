About

International Merchant Limited

International Merchants Limited is the leading manufacturer of customized furniture in Nigeria. We manufacture furniture for churches, schools, homes, offices. Furniture specifically tailored to suite your unique taste and lifestyle . We make furniture made to stand the test of time. We are also the sole distributors for the Matsunaga Stavol brand of AVR's(Automatic Voltage Regulators), manufactured in Japan, specifically for our unique power problems

INTERNATIONAL MERCHANTS LIMITED started out as a Merchandising Company before veering into importation and assembling of electronics. In 1978, The Company became the Sole Agents/Suppliers of Matsunaga ‘Stavol’ Voltage Regulators (Stabilizers) in Nigeria and the whole of West Africa and has remained so till date
